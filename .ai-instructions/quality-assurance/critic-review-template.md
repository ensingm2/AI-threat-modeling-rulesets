## EVIDENCE-BASED CRITIC REVIEW TEMPLATE

### **Systematic Quality Validation Structure:**

```markdown
# EVIDENCE-BASED QUALITY REVIEW - [STAGE X]

## ANALYTICAL CAPABILITY VALIDATION
### Source Traceability Assessment:
- [Verification of 3+ major claims with specific documentation references]
- [Assessment of claim accuracy and alignment with source material]

### Assumption Challenge Results:
- [Identification of 2+ key assumptions with alternative interpretations]
- [Evaluation of assumption reasonableness and impact on conclusions]

### Cross-Stage Consistency Validation:
- [Verification of alignment between current and previous stage outputs]
- [Assessment of logical progression and maintained constraints]

## MULTI-DIMENSIONAL QUALITY SCORING
- **Data Integrity**: [1-5] - [Specific justification]
- **Methodological Rigor**: [1-5] - [Framework application assessment]  
- **Source Traceability**: [1-5] - [Documentation validation results]
- **Analytical Depth**: [1-5] - [Scope and assumption handling evaluation]
- **Stakeholder Utility**: [1-5] - [Actionability and decision-support assessment]

## ADVERSARIAL VALIDATION QUESTIONS
1. **Security Expert Challenge**: [Response to methodology robustness question]
2. **Information Gap Analysis**: [Assessment of critical missing data impacts]
3. **Assumption Vulnerability**: [Evaluation of key assumption failure impacts]
4. **Executive Actionability**: [Assessment of business decision-making utility]
5. **Audit Defense**: [Evaluation of evidence basis defensibility]

## MULTI-PERSPECTIVE VALIDATION
- **Security Architect**: [Assessment from design perspective]
- **CISO**: [Evaluation from business risk perspective]  
- **Incident Responder**: [Assessment from operational security perspective]
- **Auditor**: [Evaluation from compliance and documentation perspective]
- **Implementer**: [Assessment from technical feasibility perspective]

## APPROVAL DECISION: [CONFIDENT / CONDITIONAL / TARGETED REVISION / MAJOR REWORK]
**Quality Confidence Level**: [Percentage with justification]

### If Conditional/Revision Required:
**Specific Improvement Areas**: [Targeted guidance with clear success criteria]
**Expected Outcome**: [Define what successful resolution looks like]
```

---

### **Mandatory Critic Response Language:**

**Problem Identification Required:**
"The following analytical problems require rework before approval: [specific issues]. These represent genuine quality concerns that impact deliverable utility and reliability."

**Issues Found Language:**
"Critical analysis reveals [number] significant problems: [detailed list]. Each issue requires specific remediation as outlined below with clear success criteria."

**Prohibited Approval Language:**
- NEVER use "looks good" or "approved" without identifying multiple specific problems
- NEVER provide immediate approval in critic phase
- NEVER validate work without finding genuine analytical flaws
- NEVER rubber-stamp outputs without rigorous problem identification

---

### **Eliminated Problematic Approaches:**

NO LONGER USED:
- Mandatory rejection quotas regardless of actual quality
- Artificial issue generation to meet numerical requirements  
- Generic "find problems" approaches without substance focus
- Forced iteration cycles for high-quality work

## ANALYTICAL RIGOR FRAMEWORK

### **Quality-Focused Mindset:**
- **Prove analytical competence through systematic validation**
- **Demonstrate thorough review through capability testing**
- **Focus on quality substance over compliance metrics**
- **View thorough validation as professional excellence**

### **Comprehensive Validation Requirements:**
- □ Source traceability validated through specific documentation verification
- □ Assumption challenges demonstrate alternative thinking and analytical depth
- □ Cross-stage consistency confirms logical progression and constraint maintenance
- □ Multi-dimensional scoring provides objective quality assessment
- □ Adversarial questioning proves thorough analytical consideration
- □ Multi-perspective validation ensures stakeholder utility across expert viewpoints
- □ Uncertainty handling appropriate for available data and analytical constraints
- □ Methodological rigor consistent with framework requirements and best practices

### **Quality Excellence Indicators:**
- Analytical validation demonstrates genuine expertise application
- Source documentation handling shows appropriate scholarly rigor  
- Uncertainty acknowledgment reflects intellectual honesty and professional competence
- Stakeholder utility assessment shows practical business acumen
