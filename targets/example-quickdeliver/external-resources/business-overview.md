# QuickDeliver - Business Overview

## What We Do

On-demand delivery app for food and stuff. Customers order, drivers deliver, merchants make money.

## Market

- **Target**: Cities with 50k+ people
- **Customers**: Busy people who want food delivered
- **Launch**: Start with 2 cities, expand later

## Competition

We're competing with DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub. Our edge:
- Lower fees for restaurants (15% vs 20-30%)
- Drivers keep more money
- Simple and fast

## Revenue

1. **Delivery Fees**: $3-7 per order
2. **Merchant Commission**: 15% of order total
3. **Subscription**: $10/month for unlimited free delivery
4. **Ads**: Restaurants pay to be featured

## Costs

- Paying drivers: 55%
- Tech/servers: 25%
- Support: 12%
- Marketing: 8%

## Value Prop

**Customers**: Fast delivery, track your order, fair prices

**Drivers**: Flexible hours, good pay (85% of fees), instant cashout

**Merchants**: Low commissions, more customers, easy setup

## Key Metrics

- Monthly active users
- Orders per day
- Average delivery time (target: 30-45 min)
- Driver and merchant retention
- Revenue per order

## Launch Plan

**Phase 1 (6 months)**: Launch in 2 cities, get 500 drivers and 200 restaurants per city

**Phase 2 (12 months)**: Expand to 5 more cities, add subscription service

**Phase 3 (24+ months)**: Go national, add grocery delivery
