# QuickDeliver - Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP (Months 1-6)

**Goal**: Launch in 2 cities with basic delivery

### Must-Have Features
- User signup and login
- Browse restaurants
- Place orders with basic customization
- Stripe payments
- Real-time order tracking with GPS
- Push notifications
- Driver app (accept deliveries, navigate, mark delivered)
- Merchant portal (receive orders, manage menu)
- Admin panel (approve drivers/merchants, view orders)

### Launch Targets
- 500 drivers per city
- 200 restaurants per city
- 1,000 orders/day across both cities

---

## Phase 2: Growth (Months 7-18)

**Goal**: Expand and make money

### New Features
- QuickDeliver Plus subscription ($10/month for free delivery)
- Instant driver payout
- Promo codes and discounts
- Schedule delivery for later
- Grocery delivery
- POS integrations (Square, Toast)
- Better analytics

### Expansion
- Add 3-5 more cities
- 50,000 orders/day total

---

## Phase 3: Scale (Months 19-36)

**Goal**: Go big

### Features
- Group orders
- Corporate accounts
- Multi-language support
- Advanced fraud detection
- Driver performance tiers with bonuses

### Expansion
- 20+ cities
- Maybe international (Canada, Mexico)
- 200,000+ orders/day
